What is it?

The Selenium Open Space Conference is organized as an Open Space “unconference” where attendees are the session contributors. That means that every session is created for and by the community members in attendance.

When is it?

Saturday, October 28th, 2023

It's not just a random day. October 28th will be the 19th anniversary of Selenium's release and we want to celebrate all day with you.
